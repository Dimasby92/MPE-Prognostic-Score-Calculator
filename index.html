Notes

GASENT age is approximated by age category. In the source publication, GASENT is a newer prognostic model for metastatic malignant pleural effusion. The clinical PROMISE score uses previous chemotherapy, previous radiotherapy, hemoglobin, white cell count, CRP, ECOG performance status, and tumor type. LENT uses pleural fluid LDH, ECOG performance status, serum NLR, and tumor type.
